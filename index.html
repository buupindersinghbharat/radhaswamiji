Our Spiritual Principles

The foundation of our spiritual practice and community

Satnam (True Name)

The eternal sound or divine name that connects the soul to the Supreme. Chanting Satnam brings inner peace and spiritual awakening.

Bhakti (Devotion)

Loving surrender to the Divine through faith and remembrance. Bhakti transforms the heart and purifies the soul.

Seva (Selfless Service)

Serving others without desire for reward is true worship. Seva connects us to humanity and dissolves the ego.
